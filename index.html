About Me

Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio site. I am currently working towards a certificate in Full Stack Development while also working as an Interactive Designer. I've been fortunate to have had the opportunity to work with exciting clients and within teams leading the creative direction on numerous initiatives and campaigns. I've always enjoyed expressing myself creatively and I feel lucky to be able to do that everyday in my career. Over the past few years i've been exploring user experience and web development and have worked towards building out those skillsets for my own professional development and interest.
